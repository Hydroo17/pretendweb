About Pretend

Pretend Bot is your comprehensive solution for managing Discord servers. Offering an array of features from moderation and music to interactive commands, Pretend is designed to enhance your server's functionality and community engagement.

Frequently Asked Questions

To invite Pretend to your server, simply click the "Invite Pretend" button located on the homepage. Alternatively, you can obtain the invite link from our Discord community.

Pretend includes a variety of features such as AutoMod for server moderation, Music for high-quality audio streaming, Tickets for support management, and Fun commands to keep your community engaged. For a detailed overview, please refer to the "Features" section above.

At this time, Pretend does not offer a premium version. All features are available at no cost to ensure accessibility for all users.

If you encounter any issues, you can report them using the report command or by reaching out to our support team through our Discord community.

Currently, we do not provide official documentation. For assistance, please connect with us through our Discord community where you can get help and support.
